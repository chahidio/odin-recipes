Sandwich

Description

Who doesn't love a good sandwich?

This marvelous culinary creation requires nothing but a fresh piece of bread and then go as crazy as you wish with the filling!

The ingredients here are merely suggestions and they are quite simple. It only takes 45 days to prepare this delightful food!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 packet sandwich seasoning
  • 1 pound mozarella cheese
  • 1 pound Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • A piece of bread

Steps

  1. Add the beef to a large skillet over medium heat and crumble as it cooks. Drain grease when cooked through.
  2. Add the sandwich seasoning to the skillet and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Slice the piece of bread in half and add the meat filling to it.
  4. Add the cheese of your choice. And that's it! Enjoy!
